Frequently Asked Questions

Q:
How do I favorite a drink
A:
Drinks can be favorited by clicking on the star, to the right of the drink name when viewing drinks individually
Q:
How can I edit a drink that I made
A:
Currently, users are unable to edit their custom drinks. Our development team is aware of this and will soon begin on implementing this feature
Q:
How do I logout
A:
There is a red logout button in the right corner, or if your screen is small there is a hamburger menu in the right corner. If you click on that you should see the red logout button. Click the logout button to logout
Q:
Can I share the drinks I create with my friends?
A:
At the moment, you cannot but this is a feature that we are working on.
Q:
Was this hard to put together.
A:
While every project has it's challenges, together with a great team we were all able to accomplish more than we originally thought we could.
